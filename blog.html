Large Antique 19th C Norwegian Tine Bentwood Folk Art Pokerwork Box Scandinavian

An antique wooden box with a distinctive Scandinavian style. The box is oval-shaped with a curved lid and a wooden handle at the top. It is constructed using the bentwood technique, where thin strips of wood are steamed and molded into shape. It was created in the 19th century.

19th Century Old Mine Cut Diamond Set Collar That Converts To Two Brackets.

This necklace positively flows around the neck and looks terrific. It is an unusual design with thirty articulated sections. Each section has been set with a mixture of old mine cut and rose diamonds. It also separates in to two bracelets, the wide section and then the narrower section. The front is silver and the reverse is gold. It was made towards the end of the 19th century.
